Buffalo Bill's XTRA CRISPY deep fried skins

Description

It rubs the flour on its skin or else it gets the hose again.

Ingredients

  • Regularly lotioned raw skins
  • Flour
  • Frying oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place the skins in a large pot.
  2. Cover with water and boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and transfer to a cutting board.
  4. Place skin on a baking sheet and refrigerate.
  5. Heat oil in a large stockpot to 175C.
  6. Fry the skins until crisp for about 4 minutes with flour.
  7. Sprinkle with salt.
