Dr. Hannibal's AMAZING Lasagna Recipe

Description

Dr. Hannibal's personal favorite for his Italian friends!

Ingredients

  • Thighs meat (skin removed) 0.2kg
  • Breast meat 0.2kg
  • Beef 0.2kg
  • Sausages 3pcs
  • Onions and garlic as you like
  • Some dried basil leaves
  • Cheese 0.2kg
  • Pasta
  • Tomato paste 3 scoops

Steps

  1. Cook the meat gravy.
  2. Boil the pasta.
  3. Layer the lasagna accordingly.
  4. Cover with tin foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
